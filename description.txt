[i/s1:997] [c/C0C0C0:Bulk Extractinator] is a Mod that allows you to extract items compatible with the 
[i/s1:997] [c/00FF7F:Extractinator] in stacks instead of one at the time.

Usage:
Open any Extractinator like a chest and insert the silt like items into the given slot and press the extract button
